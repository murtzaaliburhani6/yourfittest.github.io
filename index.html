Join the 30-Day Fit Test Challenge

No equipment. No excuses. Just 10 minutes a day to build confidence, calm, strength, and presence. For everyone.

  1. Day 1:
    - Superman Hold: Lie face down, lift arms/legs off ground. Hold 30 sec.
    - Push-Ups: Straight body, lower/lift chest. Do 10–15 reps.
    - V Sit: Sit, raise arms and legs, hold V-shape. Hold 20–30 sec.
  2. Day 2:
    - Horse Pose: Stand wide, squat halfway, hold. 30 sec.
    - Shoulder Rolls: Roll shoulders backward slowly. 10x.
    - Deep Nose Breathing: Inhale 4 sec, hold 4 sec, exhale 4 sec. 5 rounds.
  3. Day 3:
    - Shoe Shine: Quick arm swings at waist. 30 sec.
    - Cold Splash: Wash face with cold water.
    - Mirror Focus: Look into your eyes in mirror. 1 min.
  4. Day 4:
    - Jump Squats: Squat + jump. 10–15 reps.
    - Hands Behind Back: Walk slowly, hands behind. 2 mins.
    - Silent Walk: No phone, no talk, just breathe. 5 mins.
  5. Day 5:
    - Face Relax: Gently stretch face muscles.
    - Jaw Loosen: Open/close slowly, relax tension.
    - Deep Breaths: Inhale nose, exhale mouth. 10 rounds.
  6. Day 6:
    - Push-Up Hold: Lower half push-up, hold 20 sec.
    - Eye Contact (Mirror): Look at self, don’t blink. 1 min.
    - Calm Affirmation: Whisper "I’m steady. I’m calm." 5 times.
  7. Day 7:
    - Open Chest Walk: Shoulders back, walk with calm breath. 5 mins.
    - Light Smile: Carry light smile on face today.
    - Ground Check: Feel your feet while walking.
  8. Day 8:
    - Wide Stance: Stand solid, feet apart. Hold 1 min.
    - Chin Up: Tuck chin slightly, lengthen spine.
    - Calm Breath Hold: Inhale deep, hold 5 sec. Repeat x5.
  9. Day 9:
    - Quick Steps: March fast in place. 30 sec.
    - Still Face: Keep face relaxed and neutral.
    - Nose Breathing: Close mouth, breathe through nose. 2 mins.
  10. Day 10:
    - Shake Out: Shake arms, legs to release tension. 1 min.
    - Light Laugh: Laugh softly. Let it out.
    - Frame Reset: Stand still, breathe, reset posture.
  11. Day 11:
    - Wall Lean: Back to wall, breathe steady. 2 mins.
    - Mirror Gaze: Look into your eyes, no blinking. 1 min.
    - Stillness: Stand silently for 2 more mins.
  12. Day 12:
    - Low Hold: Squat halfway down, hold. 30 sec.
    - Cold Splash: Refresh face, reset mind.
    - Jaw Relax: Massage gently with fingertips.
  13. Day 13:
    - Eye Contact Drill: Hold gaze with someone or mirror. 1 min.
    - Calm Breath: Inhale, hold, exhale. 10 rounds.
    - Grounding: Feel body weight pressing down.
  14. Day 14:
    - Slow Walk: Move at 50% of normal speed.
    - Breath Sync: Match breath to steps.
    - Body Awareness: Observe posture while walking.
  15. Day 15:
    - Mirror Focus: Lock eyes with self. 1 min.
    - Whisper Calm: Say "I’m calm, I’m ready." - Spine Check: Sit/stand tall. Reset posture.
  16. Day 16:
    - Stillness: Stand still, close eyes. 3 mins.
    - Sound Awareness: Listen to all sounds around.
    - Gentle Breath: Nose only, slow and steady.
  17. Day 17:
    - Push-Up Hold: Mid-position. Hold 30 sec.
    - Breathe Only Nose: Stay calm under tension.
    - Shoulder Release: Slow backward rolls. 10x.
  18. Day 18:
    - Watch Reel: Stay stone-faced, no reaction.
    - Breathe Mindfully: Inhale nose, exhale mouth. 5 rounds.
    - Reset Face: Relax jaw, eyes, forehead.
  19. Day 19:
    - Walk Tall: Own space, chin neutral.
    - Repeat: "I move slow. I own space." - Eye Contact: Light, relaxed, respectful.
  20. Day 20:
    - Body Roll: Spine and shoulders. 1 min.
    - Shake: Arms, legs, neck. 1 min.
    - Still: Freeze in position. 1 min.
  21. Day 21:
    - Walk Market: Calm, shoulders back.
    - Light Smile: Just lips, no teeth.
    - Calm Breath: Through nose only. 5 mins.
  22. Day 22:
    - Silent Confidence: Eye contact with self/others.
    - No Talking: Hold still for 2 mins.
    - Energy Awareness: Observe how people respond.
  23. Day 23:
    - Barefoot Walk (safe place): 3 mins.
    - Feel Ground: Notice texture, sensation.
    - Breathe Deep: Calm body as you walk.
  24. Day 24:
    - Shoe Shine: 30 sec fast swings.
    - Horse Pose: Hold 30 sec.
    - Steady Gaze: Mirror or real. 1 min.
  25. Day 25:
    - Public Stillness: Stand calm in place. 2 mins.
    - Light Smile: Let go of tension.
    - Breathe Nose Only: Observe others calmly.
  26. Day 26:
    - Speak Slowly Drill: 10 slow sentences.
    - Calm Voice: No rush, full control.
    - Pause Between: 1 sec silence between thoughts.
  27. Day 27:
    - Deep Breath x10: Inhale nose, exhale mouth.
    - Chest Open: Stretch arms wide.
    - Shoulder Reset: Roll slowly 5x.
  28. Day 28:
    - Push-Up Hold: 20 sec.
    - Body Scan: Close eyes, feel head to toe.
    - Calm Breath: Match breath to scan.
  29. Day 29:
    - Choose 5 Favorite Drills – Repeat Each Once.
    - Full Focus: No distractions.
    - Journal After: 3 sentences on how you feel.
  30. Day 30:
    - Final Walk: Calm, proud, relaxed.
    - Eye Contact: Mirror or person. Hold for 1 min.
    - Combine: Top 2 favorite drills from the challenge.
